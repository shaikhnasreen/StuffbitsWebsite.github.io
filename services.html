PCB Layout

Our PCB layouts are designed in Altium Designer Studio. We have developed a rich library of 3d models for most commonly used components.

Improper component placement can lead to a horde of issues that affect functionality, durability, manufacturability, and serviceability of the PCB. Hence, we focus on best component placement practices that ensure less rejection if assembled on pick and place machines.

We also pay attention to digital vs analog signal routing and heat sinking capabilities of the PCB.
Following is the list of our PCB design skills.

  • Single layer/2 Layer/4 Layer boards
  • High Power layout adherent to IPC-2221
  • 3d modeling for Boards with Enclosure
  • Standardized component orientation for Easy Hand assembly or Wave soldering
