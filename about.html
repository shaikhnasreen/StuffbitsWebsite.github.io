Our Vision

Develop, manufacture and assemble products in India

Stuffbits was started with a vision to provide quality electronics products to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in India.
Our aim is to help these industries reduce their dependency on imports of finished Electronics goods and services.

About Us

Since commencement in 2017, our range and skills has both increased rapidly.
We started as an Embedded Firmware Development only company. There onwards we have proceeded to add various other services to our Company.

Presently we are capable of delivering a complete Electronics product from just a concept or an idea. Whether it is Hardware design, Firmware, Enclosure modeling and manufacturing or Front End for easy user interface, we provide all these services In House.
So you need not look for multiple service providers for a single product.

Keeping all product related design and developed In-House also reduces overhead costs, while ensuring smoother and faster delivery. So you are assured of great budgetary pricing , on time delivery and quality products and tools.

Work Process

Specifications and Proposal

Based on client's inputs, our work process starts with comprehensive and detailed drafting of Product Specifications.
We ensure to get an in-depth understanding of our client's requirements over the course of several technical discussions.
Post which we draft an equally thorough proposal to present what we shall be delivering.

Design and Development

Our team of talented engineers, design and develop the product in several stages.
We strive to make use of Agile development techniques for softwares and have an iterative approach to project management.
Our milestone based delivery of product in stages helps the clients to stay involved and give feedback to ensure our design meets their needs.

Testing and Validation

It's popularly know that with any Embedded Product development, testing and validation is a major part of the process.
Both off-site and on-site testing are a part of our work process to ensure the product works smoothly in industrial or normal environment.

Packaging and Delivery

Looks Matter!!! We know how packaging makes all the difference and so we also design PC user interface or Product labels with great attention to aesthetics.
Our Board Layouts are true works of art. Check out our portfolio for some of the finished products.
